Three deadlines for ChatBridge (Gauntlet Week 7):

- **Tue 2026-03-31 midnight CT:** Presearch PDF (~500-word Case Study Analysis) + 3-5 min architecture video. Planning only, no code.
- **Thu 2026-04-02 midnight CT:** Early submission — working system in draft form. NOT a hard gate for failure; purpose is for graders to give course-correction feedback. Everything required for final is due in early form EXCEPT the social post.
- **Sun 2026-04-05 midnight CT:** Final — graded harder. All deliverables including social post (X or LinkedIn, tag @GauntletAI).

**Why:** Early submission exists so graders can flag people who are way off base in time to course-correct for final. Don't treat early as pass/fail — treat it as "show enough for meaningful feedback."

**How to apply:** Prioritize having a working end-to-end flow by Thursday over polish. Final is where polish matters.
