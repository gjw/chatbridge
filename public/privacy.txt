ChatBridge Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 6, 2026

ChatBridge is an educational software project developed for academic purposes (Gauntlet AI, Week 7). It is not a commercial product.

Data We Collect
- Account information: name, email, and hashed password for platform login.
- Chat messages: conversation content stored in our database for the session.
- OAuth tokens: if you connect a Google account, we store an access token and refresh token server-side to read Google Sheets on your behalf. We request read-only access (spreadsheets.readonly).
- Usage logs: tool invocations and content filter events are logged for teacher review.

How We Use Data
- To provide the chat and app integration features of the platform.
- To filter content for student safety (K-12 use case).
- To display activity and safety dashboards for teachers/admins.

Data Sharing
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
- OAuth tokens are used only to proxy API requests to Google on your behalf. Third-party apps in the platform never see your credentials.

Data Retention
- This is a demo/academic project. Data may be reset at any time.
- You may request deletion of your data by contacting the project author.
